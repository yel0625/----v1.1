History

The Development Journey of Qilin Intelligent

1998

Establishment of Lanzhou Pingshan Machinery Factory

Started manufacturing components for pharmaceutical capsule production lines, including molds, conveyor belts, and sorting mechanisms.

  • Supplied basic components to factories in Foshan, Guangdong and Xinchang, Zhejiang
  • Collaborated with well-known enterprises such as Zhejiang Yili and Qingdao Yiqing
  • Accumulated industry experience
2004

Establishment of Lanzhou Enlin Precision Machinery Co., Ltd

Founded by engineers returning from overseas, focusing on the development of high-precision modules for medical equipment.

  • Introduction of advanced international technologies
  • Formation of a professional development team
  • Achievement of key technological breakthroughs
2010

Technological Breakthrough

Successfully developed the first fully automatic capsule production line.

  • Obtained multiple patents for key technologies
  • Started equipment export business
  • Expanded production base
2015

Establishment of Qilin Intelligent Equipment

Merged and restructured to form Gansu Qilin Intelligent Equipment Co., Ltd.

  • Integration of R&D and manufacturing resources
  • Upgrade of production facilities
  • Enhancement of market competitiveness
2020

Smart Manufacturing Upgrade

Implemented intelligent manufacturing systems and digital transformation.

  • Introduction of automated production lines
  • Development of intelligent control systems
  • Achievement of Industry 4.0 standards
2024

Global Development

Continuing to expand presence in the international market.

  • Equipment exported to over 30 countries
  • Establishment of global service network
  • Continuous technological innovation
