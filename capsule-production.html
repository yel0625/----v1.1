Hard Capsule Production Flow

The typical process of a hard capsule production line includes the following steps:

  1. Gelatin / HPMC preparation
  2. Dipping & capsule forming
  3. Trimming and cutting
  4. Drying (tumbling / tray)
  5. Sorting and defect removal
  6. Polishing
  7. Counting and packaging

Our equipment is designed to balance capacity, stability and energy consumption, making it suitable for both new plants and line upgrades.
